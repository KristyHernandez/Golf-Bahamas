Championship Golf Course

by Legendary Pro-Golfer Greg Norman

Located at the Bahamas' Leading Golf Resort, Greg Norman's award-winning golf course design at Sandals Emerald Bay preserves
the landscape's pristine beauty and natural barriers. As the longest course in the Caribbean, winding through seaside dunes along
a stunning peninsula, this 18-hole, par 72 championship course is consistently regarded as one of the best in the Caribbean by
Travel + Leisure Golf. Sandals Emerald Bay Golf Course is the proud host of the Bahamas Great Exuma Classic, a PGA
TOUR-sanctioned Korn Ferry Tour event.
